Stage 4 — On-site survey

Record findings — minimum taps

The surveyor works through the property. Observations are captured as structured taps, not typing — record it once and the software writes the report. The capture form adapts to the survey type. Drop a numbered marker on the plan for each finding.

Tap the plan to pin a finding · drag a pin to the exact room & wall
Pins use % coordinates, so they map correctly on any screen or the client's device.

Stage 5 — AI drafting

Building the report

Everything the surveyor captured is sent — server-side — to the Claude API, which turns the structured survey into a full report: key findings, professional opinions and a matched schedule of works, written in Damp Experts Group's own voice, learned from hundreds of past reports. The API key never touches the browser.

1 · Survey data collected on site → sent to Claude
2 · Claude generates the report from that data
Stage 6 — Review & send

Review, price & self-approve

The surveyor checks the AI draft, edits any wording, enters the price manually, then approves and sends — no office gate. Everything flows straight into the client's report and email.
